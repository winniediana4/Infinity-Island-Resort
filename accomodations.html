STAY WITH US

Our spacious and luxurious rooms are all ensuite and individually decorated using beautiful colours and art giving each room a personalized character. They all have access to a balcony offering a breathtaking view of the sunrise and sunset overlooking the surrounding hills.

All beds are spread with soft and freshly-scented linen that will give you a good night's sleep. The bathroom ammenities are all provided, which include hot water to ensure your ultimate comfort.

Our accomodation include:

  • Double Rooms

    • Elegantly furnished with an African Swahili touch, our spacious double rooms feature a King-size bed with plush pillows that will let you experience a truly memorable stay as you sink into lavish, sleek, white beddings. This is ideal for both single occupancy or for couples.

  • Twin Room

    • Relax and rest easy with two comfortable beds provided. This option allows you to share with a family member or a friend.

  • Family Room

    • Our family room features a double room that connects to a twin room making them ideal for large families or groups of four.

  • Honeymoon Room

    • The room offers a romantic and elegant ambience to celebrate your first night together and cheers to many more with complimentary wine, flowers and chocolate provided.
