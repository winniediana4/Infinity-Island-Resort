Activities

Whether you are alone, in a group, a couple or family, Infinity Island Resorts offers an incredible array of activities. From swimming pool with slides to massages, kids' quad bikes, bouncing castles, archery, bonfires and many more. Our team is more than pleased to organise all of these.

You can also choose to read a book, relaxing as you enjoy the scenery, considering how peaceful and serene this environment is. If you are lucky you will spot wild animals grazing and roaming about.
